Why Tokyo?

Tokyo, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples

The capital city of Japan, is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant mix of modernity and tradition. It stands as one of the world's most populous and influential cities. Tokyo is famous for its towering skyscrapers, cutting-edge technology, and a fast-paced urban lifestyle. Yet, amidst the modernity, you can find serene parks, historic temples, and a rich cultural heritage.

Coffee

My favorite coffee shop in Tokyo

Hatcoffee

As long as there’s steamed milk, latte art master Kohei Matsuno can transform any ordinary coffee into a tiny work of art. So show him a photo of your favourite character or animal and he’ll recreate it on the drink’s surface either as a flat or 3D rendition right in front of your eyes. Otherwise, just opt for the basic latte art and leave it to Matsuno’s imagination. Either way, you won’t be disappointed. Note that reservations are required and can be made online.

Adress:

3 Chome-15-6 Kotobuki, Taito City, Tokyo 111-0042, Japan

Pikachu Sweets Cafe

"Pikachu Sweets Café" is a Japanese café dedicated to the beloved Pokémon character, Pikachu. It serves a range of Pikachu-themed sweets and beverages, making it a must-visit for Pokémon fans and those seeking adorable treats in a fun, themed atmosphere.
